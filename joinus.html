Become a Member

Why Join Our Book Club?

Join a community of passionate readers who meet monthly to discuss thought-provoking books across all genres. Whether you're a casual reader or a literary enthusiast, our club offers a welcoming space to share insights, discover new perspectives, and make lasting friendships through the love of reading.

Membership Benefits

As a member, you'll enjoy exclusive access to our monthly book selections, participate in engaging discussions led by fellow members, receive discounts on book purchases through our shop, and get first priority for special author events and literary workshops. Connect with like-minded readers and expand your literary horizons!

How to Join

Joining is easy! Simply fill out the membership form below with your contact information and let us know what interests you about our book club. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with membership details, our upcoming meeting schedule, and information about our next book selection. There are no membership fees - just a shared love of reading!

Meeting Schedule

We meet on the first Thursday of every month at 7:00 PM in the Central Library Meeting Room B (1234 Reading Lane, Booktown, GA 30000). Meetings typically last 90 minutes and include refreshments, lively discussion, and time to socialize. Can't make it in person? We also offer virtual attendance options!
